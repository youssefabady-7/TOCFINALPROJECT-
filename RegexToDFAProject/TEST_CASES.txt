Professor Demo Test Case
========================

Purpose:
Show the complete flow from regular expression to NFA, DFA, minimized DFA,
and string testing.

How to run:
1. Run Main.java.
2. Choose option 6: Run professor demo test case.

Demo regular expression:
(a|b)*abb

Language:
All strings over {a,b} that end with abb.

Expected accepted strings:
abb
aabb
bababb

Expected rejected strings:
ab
aba
bbb

Custom testing:
After the demo runs, choose option 5 to test your own strings on the loaded
minimized DFA.

Custom regular expressions:
Choose option 1 at any time to enter your own regular expression.
